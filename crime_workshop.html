Crime Data Workshop

This workshop introduces officers to modern data tools used in crime analysis. Learn how to interpret trends, spot criminal patterns, and use predictive policing tools to improve response strategies.

Participants will engage in live demos, case studies, and hands-on sessions with data dashboards. By the end, officers will be equipped to make data-informed decisions for crime prevention. The workshop will be held online via Google Meet, starting from August 12, 2025, with daily sessions from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The access link will be shared one day prior to the workshop through email.
